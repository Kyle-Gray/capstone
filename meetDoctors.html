Doctor Nathan Irwin, D.C.

Dr. Nathan Irwin DC has been helping patients become healthier and happier for many years now. A graduate of Palmer College of Chiropractic, Davenport, Dr. Irwin founded Irwin Family Chiropractic 7 years ago in hopes of treating his patients with a "whole body approach" and family oriented mindset. He has now incorporated physical medicine and numerous other exciting treatment styles into his office, thus founding Advanced Healthcare. His patients can now enjoy a wide variety of unique and individualized treatments to help them achieve their goals.

Doctor Rob Smigelski, D.C.

After serving over 7 years in the Navy as a flight navigator, he was introduced to chiropractic medicine and learned the value of alternative medicine for health and wellness He attended Palmer College of Chiropractic where he excelled in his class, becoming a Presidential Scholar and earning membership into the Vogt Leadership Society. He received his Doctor of Chiropractic degree in 1998 where he graduated summa cum laude In addition to chiropractic, he specializes in functional medicine, and recently earned his Masters of Acupuncture degree from the Institute of Taoist Education and Acupuncture in Louisville, CO. Dr. Smigelski believes everyone should have the ability to live their lives to the fullest, and that chiropractic can play an important role in achieving that. He views his patients not just as a person with a condition, but as a whole person, capable of achieving and maintaining optimal health. His holistic view encourages healing through care that focuses on the individual, preventing illness and promoting health and wellness. In his free time, he loves to spend time with his family, play hockey and is working toward his third-degree black belt in martial arts.
